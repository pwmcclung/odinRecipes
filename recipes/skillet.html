Skillet Cheeseburger Pasta

Description:

This skillet cheeseburger dish will make you nostalgic for grilling season. Just a few simple ingredients and a little prep time and you have a tremendously flavorful dish that's guaranteed to please!

Ingredients:

  • 2 teaspoons butter
  • 2 teaspoons oil
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 1/4 lbs lean ground beef
  • 1 (12 oz) can evaporated milk
  • 1 eight ounce can tomato sauce
  • 2 tablespoons pickle relish
  • 2 table spoons dijon mustard
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 cups beef broth
  • 1 1/2 cups macaroni
  • 3 cups shredded cheese

Directions:

  1. Melt butter with oil in a deep cast iron skillet over medium heat. Add chopped onion and cook until translucent, about 3 minutes.
  2. Add minced garlic and ground beef; cook and stir, breaking up the meat with a spatula, until beef is crumbly and browned, about 7 minutes.
  3. Stir in tomato sauce and evaporated milk. Add pickle relish, Dijon mustard, granulated garlic, granulated onion, Montreal steak seasoning, chili powder, smoked paprika, salt, and pepper, and stir to combine.

  5. Stir in dry macaroni until pasta is fully submerged. Bring mixture to a boil, reduce the heat to low, and simmer, covered, until macaroni is tender with a bite, about 10 minutes.
